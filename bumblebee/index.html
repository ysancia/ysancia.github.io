Bumblebee

3yo boy: [whining]
Me: What's wrong?
3yo boy: I want bumblebee!
Me: We don't have a bumblebee toy.
3yo boy: Yes we do! I can't find it!

(After a brief moment of reflection)

Me: Oh, do you mean this toy (a musical shaker shaped like a bee)?
3yo boy: No! I want the bumblebee with a gun!
Me: A gun?
3yo boy: The Transformer!
Me: OHHH. You meant Bumblebee the Transformer...
